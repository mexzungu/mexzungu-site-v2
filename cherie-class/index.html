Chérie Class Programme

Terms & Conditions of Enrolment

Authorised by The Captain  ·  Third Orbit, Barcelona Sector

These Terms and Conditions govern the Chérie Class Programme (the "Programme"), operated by Intergalactic Pepe (the "Company"), an independent, free-spirited cosmic travel company, incorporated under the laws of Mars, with representative offices on the island of Mauritius and the city of Barcelona. The Programme is administered by The Captain, sole operator and pilot of the Company. By accepting any benefit under the Programme, the enrolled passenger (the "Cherie") is deemed to have read, understood, and accepted these Terms in their entirety. Ignorance of these Terms is noted and found charming. It does not constitute a defence.

1. Eligibility

Chérie Class status is issued at the sole and absolute discretion of The Captain following a personal review of the passenger's flight history and cosmic compatibility. Selection criteria are proprietary, non-disclosable, and not subject to inquiry or appeal. Status cannot be applied for. It is conferred. The Company makes no representations, warranties, or apologies in this regard.

2. Duration

The Programme is valid for thirty (30) calendar days from the date of issue. The validity period commences upon notification by The Captain. Activation constitutes full acceptance of these Terms. The universe witnesses it.

3. Frequency

The Cherie is entitled to up to two (2) visits per week during the validity period. Additional visits may be arranged at The Captain's discretion, subject to orbital availability. The Captain does not guarantee availability but acknowledges, on the record, that responsiveness will be unreasonably prompt.

4. Benefits

During the validity period, the Cherie is entitled to the following: priority boarding; extended layovers; in-flight catering prepared by The Captain; access to classified cabin features not available in standard class; personalised navigation assistance for the duration of the flight; oral appreciation services administered with precision and dedication; balcony access for atmospheric encounters; admission to select tantra temple events at The Captain's discretion; complimentary cuddles, both pre and post-flight; and access to a curated programme of questionable decisions, the consequences of which are shared equally between parties. Benefits are personal, non-transferable, and not combinable with any competing orbital arrangements.

5. Nature of Upgrade

The Chérie Class upgrade is complimentary. No financial consideration is exchanged. No contractual obligation arises beyond the duration specified in Clause 2. The Company provides no warranties as to outcomes, including but not limited to: unexpected contentment, difficulty returning to orbit, or permanently recalibrated expectations. These are features, not liabilities.

6. Governing Law

This Programme is governed exclusively by the laws of Mars (Third Orbit, Barcelona Sector), as interpreted by The Captain. Earth-side legal frameworks — including but not limited to the laws of France, Spain, the European Union, and any other jurisdiction in the known solar system — are noted and, with due respect, do not apply. The Company extends this position without reservation.

7. Independence

The Chérie Class Programme is entirely independent of any applications previously submitted to the Martian Embassy or processed by the Intergalactic Lover Selection Committee. Prior proceedings under those frameworks are neither superseded nor affected by this Programme. Each framework operates on its own orbital trajectory. The Company takes no position on those matters.

8. Amendments

The Captain reserves the right to amend these Terms at any time, subject to reasonable notice delivered in person or via encrypted transmission. Amendments take effect upon issuance. Continued receipt of benefits following notification of an amendment constitutes acceptance. The Captain considers this the most meaningful form of consent.

9. Expiry and Renewal

Upon expiry of the thirty-day validity period, the Cherie may apply for renewal. Renewal is not automatic. It is subject to a new review by The Captain, conducted at a time of The Captain's choosing. Prior status does not constitute a guarantee of renewal. The Captain observes, deliberates, and decides. Decisions are not subject to lobbying. Being oneself is, however, more persuasive than lobbying and is accordingly encouraged.

10. Acceptance

Acceptance of any benefit under the Programme — including but not limited to priority boarding, extended layovers, in-flight catering, or access to classified cabin features — constitutes full and unconditional acceptance of these Terms and Conditions in their entirety. No signature is required. The Captain will note it in the file.

11. Acceptance Protocol

Formal acceptance of the Cherie Class upgrade shall be ratified in accordance with the Martian Acceptance Rite (Third Edition). The ceremony is brief, conducted at an appropriate altitude, and requires The Cherie to present herself in the prescribed position: knees on the launchpad, mouth open to receive the Captain's blessing. The details of this rite are classified under Martian privacy law but are generally well understood by all parties involved. Completion of the rite constitutes unconditional acceptance of all terms herein.

Schedule Your Acceptance Ceremony

Ceremony subject to Captain availability. 24h notice required. Dress code: flexible.
