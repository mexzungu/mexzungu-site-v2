Who this is for

You built something real.

You built something real — by instinct, by passion, by showing up. People come. They come back. But the back end is chaos, nobody owns anything, and you are exhausted from holding it all together. Root & Wing is for you.

How it works

Three acts. One through-line.

01

The Mirror

We look at what you have built and reflect it back clearly. What is working. What is creating friction. What needs a decision. You receive a visual document — The Mirror — before we meet. The session is for alignment, not discovery.

02

The Nest

We build the structure together. Not for you — with you. Ops that run without heroic effort. Roles that are clear. A story people understand before they arrive. A community that grows instead of just sustaining. Four phases, conservative pacing, one milestone at a time.

03

The Flight

You fly solo. The engagement ends when you no longer need us to hold the structure. That is the definition of done — not dependency, independence.

The four phases of The Nest
  • 1
    Weeks 1–2

    The Clearing

    Clarity session. Value proposition, roles, key decisions.

  • 2
    Weeks 3–8

    The Ground

    Operational structure. Fix what creates friction.

  • 3
    Weeks 9–14

    The Voice

    Story and communications. People know what they are signing up for.

  • 4
    Weeks 15–24

    The Bloom

    Community and growth. Intentional, not accidental.

Not every engagement needs all four phases. The Mirror tells us where to start.

The exchange

No fees. No equity.

Root & Wing is barter-based. Pepe brings the structure. You bring access to what you do — classes, sessions, events, experiences, introductions. The exchange is agreed before the work begins. Never after.

What Pepe asks for is always proportional to what you do.
And always something he actually wants.
