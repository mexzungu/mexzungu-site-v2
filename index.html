Track Record
We don't talk theory. We've done the work.
Venture Architecture

Scaling across 10 countries, 3 continents

A pan-African education founder had the vision to transform the continent by developing 3 million ethical entrepreneurial leaders — but no structural backbone to expand beyond a couple of tiny markets. Every new country meant a new regulatory maze, a new entity puzzle, a new way for things to break. I built the architecture that let them move into 5 new markets without slowing down, from holdco design to local compliance to fundraising mechanics. Over $500M raised and operations running across 10 countries on 3 continents.

Corporate Transactions

$6B+ in cross-border deals

The largest cross-border RMBS in Latin America. First-of-their-kind private-equity-gone-public bonds. Bank mergers and tech acquisitions. Solar and wind farm takeovers. Every one of them layered, political, and full of landmines that only show up at 2am on a Saturday — and every one of them closed. Over a decade of wild and crazy deals across multiple continents, for the likes of Goldman Sachs, ENGIE, and Nissan, adding up to over $6 billion in completed transactions.
