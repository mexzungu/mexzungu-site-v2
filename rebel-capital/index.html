Investment Thesis
Backing builders from the ALG network and beyond.

I invest in founders I know — primarily from the African Leadership Group ecosystem and adjacent networks. People who've operated in hard markets, built with constraints, and have the grit to make it work.

Founder-first

I back people, not pitch decks. The founders I invest in have operated in the real world — they know what hard looks like.

Africa & emerging markets

The opportunities are enormous and underserved. I invest where I have context, relationships, and operational understanding.

Structural edge

Most angels write a cheque and disappear. I bring venture architecture — ownership design, governance, regulatory positioning — from day one.

Long-term conviction

Small tickets, big commitment. I'm not optimising for portfolio theory. I'm in it because I believe in what you're building.

Beyond Capital
What founders actually get.

The cheque is the smallest part. What matters is what comes with it.

  • Board or advisory seat — active, not decorative
  • Structural and legal consulting as you scale
  • Governance design when investors start showing up
  • Cap table and ownership architecture from the start
  • Access to a network of operators across Africa, LatAm, and beyond
  • Someone who picks up the phone when it matters

What I Look For
Not a checklist. A gut call.

But if I had to write it down:

Founders who've been in the arena

Prior operating experience. Built something before. Understand the difference between a pitch and a plan.

Solving real problems

Not chasing trends. Building for markets they understand deeply, solving problems they've lived.

Capital efficiency

Can do a lot with a little. Understand that runway is earned, not given.

Structural ambition

Want to build something that lasts. Not a flip — a company with real architecture underneath.
