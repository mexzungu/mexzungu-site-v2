Privacy Policy

Who we are

Mexzungu Group Limited ("MGL") is a consulting studio operated by Pepe Carrillo. Our website is mexzungu.com.

What data we collect

This website does not use cookies, analytics trackers, or third-party scripts that collect personal data. We do not collect, store, or process visitor data beyond standard server logs maintained by our hosting provider (Cloudflare Pages).

Internal tools

MGL uses internal automation tools ("Janis") that access Google Workspace APIs (Gmail, Drive, Calendar, Contacts) solely for the business operations of MGL and its founder. These tools:

  • Access only accounts owned and controlled by Pepe Carrillo
  • Do not access, collect, or process data from third-party users
  • Store OAuth tokens locally on secured, encrypted infrastructure
  • Do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties

Messaging and conversation data

When you communicate with MGL via WhatsApp or Telegram, Janis may process your messages to provide responses on behalf of MGL. This processing involves:

  • Storing conversation history in a local database to maintain context across messages
  • Routing messages through Anthropic's Claude AI service for response generation
  • Retaining conversation data for up to 90 days, after which it is purged
  • Using conversation context only within the same session or for continuity across channels

Conversation data is not used for training AI models, is not shared with third parties beyond the AI service provider (Anthropic), and is stored on infrastructure within the European Union. The legal basis for this processing is legitimate interest (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)) — namely, providing efficient client communications.

Data retention

Conversation data: 90 days. OAuth tokens: retained while accounts are active. Server logs (Cloudflare): per Cloudflare's retention policy. We do not retain personal data longer than necessary for the purposes described above.
