About

Rigger. Model. Teacher.

Guinju is a Barcelona-based shibari rigger, model, and educator with roots in the traditional Japanese rope arts of kinbaku. His practice sits at the intersection of aesthetics, trust, and physical presence, where constraint becomes communication.

Trained in both the technical and the philosophical dimensions of rope, Guinju teaches shibari not as performance, but as dialogue. Each tie is a conversation. Each knot, a question asked and answered in silence.

Whether in private sessions, group workshops, or behind the lens, his work explores what it means to truly see and be seen, bound and free at once. Featured in collaborations with studios across Barcelona including La Gabia and Flashe Studio.
