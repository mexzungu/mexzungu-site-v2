About

I'm Pepe. I build things.

I'm a legal strategist and venture architect. I operate at the intersection of law, capital, and business growth across Africa, Latin America, and Europe.

I started in big law. Goldman Sachs, ENGIE, Nissan. Over $6 billion in corporate transactions across multiple jurisdictions. The kind of work where a misplaced comma can blow up a deal and a late night is just a Tuesday.

Then I went to Africa. At the African Leadership Group I closed more than $500M in fundraising, helped scale operations across 10 countries on 3 continents, and launched commercial expansion into 5 new markets. That's where everything changed. I stopped being a lawyer who advises companies and started being an architect who builds them.

Today I run Mexzungu Group. It's an outlaw consulting studio. The place where legal clarity meets creative fire, where strategy meets rebellion, and where wild founders get the structure they need without losing the spark that makes them dangerous.
