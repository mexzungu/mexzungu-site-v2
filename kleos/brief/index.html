Engagement Roadmap

A 24-week arc — from clarity to community.

The Plan

How We Do This

Conservative pacing. One milestone at a time. Not every engagement needs all four phases — the clarity session tells us where to start and what to skip.

Weeks 1–2

01

The Clearing

Clarity and foundation. Lock the value proposition, agree on roles, resolve the open questions. Leave with a shared compass.

  • Value proposition in one sentence
  • Roles and decision authority confirmed
  • Open strategic questions resolved
  • Shared compass for the months ahead

Weeks 3–8

02

The Ground

Operational setup. Fix what is creating friction without slowing what is already working.

  • Registration flow simplified
  • Basic revenue tracking live
  • Communications templates built
  • Roles and ownership clearly defined

Weeks 9–14

03

The Voice

Story and communications. People know what they are signing up for before they arrive. Fear becomes curiosity.

  • One explainer per event type
  • First-timer guide
  • Consistent channel voice established
  • Brand language documented

Weeks 15–24

04

The Bloom

Community and growth. Intentional, not accidental. The platform starts to attract without heroic effort from the founders.

  • Community activation plan in place
  • Practitioner onboarding process
  • First expanded offering or retreat
  • Growth without founder dependency
