Why does this letter exist?

Because the shape of this engagement is worth getting right in writing. We have talked about the ambition behind Silica GBS, what you are building, and where the Studio fits. This letter captures the terms: what you are getting, at which level, what it costs, how we pay each other, and what happens if anything ever needs to change. Read it, select your tier, sign it, and let us get to work.

Where does the Studio sit, geographically and legally?

The Studio is a Mauritius outfit. We work out of our registered office on the island and deliver from there to wherever Silica GBS is operating. Pepe signs as Chief Disruption Officer. None of what follows means we have set up shop, opened a branch, or started practising law in any jurisdiction you operate in. We are the structural architects and strategic operators in your corner, not local lawyers of record.

1. What are we working on together?

Silica GBS is building a pan-African business process outsourcing and GBS platform, targeting 30 or more delivery centres and a talent base of 100,000 people. The AI-augmented talent model is the core differentiator. The challenge is that the structural, legal, and commercial architecture needs to scale as fast as the ambition does. That is where the Studio comes in.

This is a monthly retainer engagement. You select the level that fits where Silica GBS is right now, and we work within that level every month. The level can be upgraded or downgraded with 30 days written notice. The three tiers are described in section 3 below and selected in the activation section before your signature.

2. What are we not doing?

(a) Filing anything with any court, registry, notary, or government office, anywhere.
(b) Being your lawyer of record in any jurisdiction Silica GBS operates in.
(c) Replacing your local lawyers. For filing, litigation, or jurisdiction-specific execution, you will need qualified local counsel. We can point you to good people.
(d) Anything outside the scope of the tier you have selected. If a need falls outside your tier, we tell you that clearly and discuss whether to expand scope or upgrade.

3. What does each tier cover?

Select the tier that matches where you are. You get exactly what is in the tier, nothing more, nothing less. The tiers are described in the selection section below, but the substance of each is as follows.

Tier 1 — On Call ($4,000 / month). Async structural support via Janis (the Studio's AI-powered Chief of Staff), available 24/7 for questions, drafts, and reviews. One strategic call per month with Pepe (60 minutes). Single-jurisdiction focus. Kellen available for administrative tasks. 30-day written notice to exit. Best for early-stage engagements, specific workstreams, or founders who need a smart back-office without the full weight of a retained advisor.
Tier 2 — Structural Partner ($6,000 / month). Everything in Tier 1. Two-jurisdiction coverage. Bi-weekly 45-minute check-ins with Pepe. Quarterly participation in a board or investor meeting (observer mode). Pepe responds within 4 CET business hours on items flagged urgent. Contract drafting and review included within scope. Best for companies actively structuring across multiple markets or preparing for a capital raise.
Tier 3 — In the Room ($8,000 / month). Everything in Tiers 1 and 2. Multi-jurisdiction coverage with no cap. Weekly 30-minute check-in with Pepe. Monthly participation in a board meeting. Janis responds within the hour on anything. Pepe responds within 4 CET business hours, always. Active deal flow structuring included. Investor preparation materials (decks, term sheet analysis, data room structure) included as part of monthly scope. Best for companies in active deal execution, scaling across multiple jurisdictions simultaneously, or where speed and availability matter.

4. Is this legal advice?

No. This is legal consulting and strategic advisory. We give you the architecture, the analysis, the playbook, and the drafts. We do not file papers, sign legal opinions, or appear in court. Think of us as the friend who has closed a few billion in deals across a bunch of countries, sitting next to you at the table, telling you what to look out for and how to structure it cleanly.

6. What happens if something goes wrong?

If something goes sideways and you come after us, the most we are on the hook for is the cumulative fees you have actually paid us at that point. Not hypothetical future fees, not consequential losses. The two things we cannot contractually cap are fraud and wilful misconduct. We are not planning on either.

7. Who else sees what we discuss?

Nobody. What happens in this engagement stays in this engagement. You do not share our memos, analysis, or thinking outside this engagement without our permission. We do not share your business, structure, or strategy with anyone. This commitment does not expire when the engagement does.

8. Can the Studio talk about this work later?

Yes, in general terms. Once everything is public or done, we might say something like "we supported a pan-African GBS platform through a major scaling phase." We will never name Silica GBS, share your documents, or say anything specific without your written permission.

9. How long does this last?

This is a month-to-month engagement. It starts on the date at the top. Either party may terminate by giving 30 days written notice to the other. No penalties, no clawbacks. Any fees earned and unpaid at termination remain due and payable. Confidentiality survives termination indefinitely.

The tier level can be upgraded at any time with immediate effect (subject to mutual agreement). Tier downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing month following 30 days written notice.

10. Which law applies if we ever disagree?

Mauritius law. If we cannot sort it out between ourselves, it goes to the Supreme Court of Mauritius (Commercial Division). We are not expecting to need this. It is here so neither of us ever has to wonder.
