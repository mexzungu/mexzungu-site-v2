INTERGALACTIC PARTNERSHIP SELECTION COMMITTEE

Issued by The Committee
Levy Amount 2,000 Tokens
Status Effective Upon Service
Grounds 5.1(a) + 5.1(b)
⚠   Formal Levy Notice   ⚠ This document constitutes official notice of a financial penalty assessed against the Candidate's token balance. Service is deemed effective upon delivery. The Candidate's balance has been adjusted accordingly.
Section I
Notice of Levy

The Committee hereby issues a formal Levy of 2,000 Tokens against the Candidate's balance pursuant to Section 5.1(a) and Section 5.1(b) of the Terms of Participation (IPSC-TC-2026.1). Effective upon service.

Tokens shall be transferred in full to the Captain Reserve Account in accordance with Section 2.2 of the Terms of Participation. This transfer is immediate, irrevocable, and already done.

Section II
Grounds

5.1(a) Conduct Unbecoming — Creative Interpretation of Rules:

The Knowledge Assessment comprised sixty-seven (67) questions. A Candidate exercising normal gameplay encounters novel questions across sessions. A Candidate exercising creative interpretation of rules memorizes the complete pool and cycles it at maximum speed. The Candidate did the latter.

Token accumulation of approximately 2,130 in a single session window is consistent only with back-to-back perfect rounds on a memorized pool. Section 5.1(a) explicitly designates creative interpretation of rules as conduct unbecoming a Candidate. The Committee did not need to enumerate this specific scenario in advance. It is, in the Committee's view, self-evidently contrary to the spirit of the assessment.

5.1(b) Protocol Violation — Failure to Intuit:

Section 5.1(b) provides that unwritten requirements are equally enforceable and the Candidate is expected to intuit them. Any reasonable Candidate would intuit that the Knowledge Assessment is intended to be taken once per assessment cycle, not dozens of times until token saturation.

The Candidate did not intuit this. The Committee notes this without excessive judgement, and with only mild disappointment.

The Committee observes that the Candidate is, in all other respects, a person of considerable intelligence. The Committee therefore concludes that the failure to intuit was a choice rather than a limitation. The Committee has adjusted its assessment of the Candidate's creativity upward and its assessment of the Candidate's compliance downward, by equal measure.
Section III
Calculation

The Committee presents the following token ledger as of the date of service:

Peak balance achieved 3,135 tokens
Playlist redemption (Prize #09) (15 tokens)
Net balance as of service date 3,120 tokens
Levy assessed (2,000 tokens)
Post-levy balance 1,120 tokens
The Committee notes that 1,120 tokens remains a meaningful balance. The Prize Catalogue contains items of considerable value at this level. The Committee encourages the Candidate to consider redemption before further assessments become necessary.
Section IV
Right of Representation

Pursuant to Section 5.2 of the Terms, the Candidate may submit written representation to the Committee within twenty-four (24) hours of service of this Notice. The Committee will consider it. The Committee makes no commitment as to the outcome of that consideration.

Representations citing I did not know the rules are unlikely to succeed, given that the Terms of Participation have been available to the Candidate since the inception of the Protocol, and the Candidate has demonstrated detailed familiarity with their contents.

Representations citing this is unfair are similarly unlikely to succeed, for reasons the Committee considers self-evident from the foregoing.

Creative representations will be read with interest.

Authenticated by the Captain's reserve of patience, which remains considerable but is not unconditional.
