Having reviewed the complete dossier and conducted three rounds of deliberation, the Committee issues the following ruling. It is final. It is not subject to counter-proposal, amendment, or appeal. The Candidate may respond with silence, gratitude, or both.
Exhibit A — The Polyamory Manuscript

Received 22 April 2026. The apology for the 2021 prose was noted. It was dismissed. The Committee does not award consideration for acknowledging one's own failures. The manuscript demonstrated something. The Committee will not say what. It was sufficient.

Exhibit B — The Counter-Proposal

Ten clauses. Nine ratified. Clause 3 (CDI) deferred. This is not a locked door. It is a matter the Committee reserves for in-person deliberation. The Candidate should understand the difference between a door that is locked and one that opens from the inside.

Exhibit C — The Meeting Documentation

Four hours of notes, unsolicited, delivered without prompting. Initiative was observed. So was the excess of it. The Committee will not elaborate. The Candidate may infer.

Exhibit D — The Ohgasm Preparatory Materials

Reviewed. Classified. The Committee will address this in person. The discussion it generated during deliberation will not be disclosed.

  • I. The Candidate is admitted to Chérie Class, effective immediately upon receipt of this ruling.

  • IV. All nine ratified counter-proposal clauses are incorporated into the terms of admission without further negotiation.
