What You Need to Know

What is virtual domination?
Virtual domination is a form of BDSM (Bondage, Discipline, Dominance, Submission, Sadism, Masochism) conducted entirely online — typically via video call, messaging, or audio. The psychological and emotional elements of power exchange are fully present even without physical contact. Many practitioners find that virtual sessions allow for deeper vulnerability, greater access, and more consistent engagement than in-person sessions. It is a legitimate, widely practiced form of consensual adult interaction.
Is this safe and consensual?
Absolutely. Consent is the bedrock of everything I do. Before any session, we conduct a detailed negotiation covering your interests, hard limits, soft limits, safewords, and any relevant health or psychological considerations. Nothing happens without your explicit, enthusiastic, and ongoing consent. You may use your safeword at any time to pause or end a scene — no questions asked, no pressure. Safe, Sane, and Consensual (SSC) is the operating principle of this community.
What happens in a private session?
Each private session begins with a pre-session consultation (usually by email or brief call) where we discuss your interests, negotiate the scene, establish safewords, and set expectations. The 60-minute live session follows via secure video platform. Post-session, there is an optional debrief to process the experience and discuss what worked or what you would like to explore differently next time. Every session is unique, tailored entirely to the dynamic we establish together.
How does the Inner Circle membership work?
The Inner Circle is a monthly subscription at $39/month, billed automatically, cancelable anytime. Upon joining, you receive immediate access to the full content library, an invitation to the private members' forum, and a welcome message from me directly. Every month, I host a live group session — typically 90 minutes — which includes a guided discussion, Q&A, and sometimes a structured group exercise. Members also receive early access to book private sessions and first notice of new content drops.
How do I book a private session?
Submit an inquiry using the form on this page. Include a brief introduction, the service you are interested in, and any relevant context about your experience or interests. I respond to all inquiries within 48 hours. New clients will receive a short consultation questionnaire — this helps me understand you and ensures our session will be genuinely suited to both of us. Once consultation is complete, I will provide available dates and payment instructions to confirm your booking.
What is your cancellation and refund policy?
Private sessions may be rescheduled with a minimum of 24 hours' notice at no charge. Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice are non-refundable. Late cancellations with exceptional circumstances (genuine emergencies) are reviewed case by case — I am a human being and I understand that life happens. Inner Circle memberships may be cancelled at any time; your access continues until the end of the current billing period, after which no further charges are made. No partial refunds are issued for unused membership time.
